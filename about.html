Our mission as first time hackathon programmers is to help stop the spread of misinformation on the internet. We made a fact checker that uses the Wikimedia API in order to search through and retrieve articles to determine whether a statement is true or false. If the statement could not be evaluated as true or false, it gives links to articles with potentially relevant information. In the results, articles with the most mentions of the search term are ordered first.
